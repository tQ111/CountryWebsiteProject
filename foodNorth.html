Known for being salty, sweet, and sour

---Braised chicken---

> Braised chicken is a tender chicken dish cooked in a rich,
savory sauce with soy, spices, and aromatics, often paired with
vegetables.

---Sweet and Sour Carp---

A crispy-fried whole carp coated in a glossy sweet and sour
sauce, balancing tangy and sugary notes.

---Diced Pork Cubes---

Small, flavorful diced pork cubes stir-fried with vegetables,
often seasoned with soy sauce and spices for a hearty dish.

---Braised Prawns---

Fresh braised prawns cooked in a luscious soy-based sauce,
highlighting their natural sweetness and rich umami flavors.

---Steamed Stuffed Tofu---

Soft steam stuffed tofu filled with a savory meat or seafood
mixture, then steamed to perfection and served with a light sauce.
